FAQs - Hiranandani Sands Alibaug

Where is Hiranandani Sands Alibaug located?

Hiranandani Sands is located at Nagaon Beach, Alibaug, Maharashtra—one of the most sought-after coastal destinations near Mumbai.

What type of project is Hiranandani Sands?

It is a premium plotted development where you can build your own custom luxury villa.

What is the starting price of plots?

Plots at Hiranandani Sands start from ₹2.65 Cr onwards, depending on size and location.

What plot sizes are available?

The project offers plots ranging from 1500 to 3500 sq.ft to suit different needs.

What is the total land area of the project?

The project is spread across a massive 225-acre master-planned township.

What amenities are included in the project?

Residents get access to a mega clubhouse, wetland park, private jetty, water sports, and landscaped open spaces.

How is the connectivity to Mumbai?

The project is well connected via Mandwa Jetty (~35 mins) and upcoming infrastructure like the Virar–Alibaug corridor.

Is the project RERA registered?

Yes, Hiranandani Sands is a RERA-registered project, ensuring transparency and compliance.

Can I visit the site before booking?

Yes, site visits and virtual tours are available with prior booking.
